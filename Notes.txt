All following notes are for World of Warcraft (not Classic)

Regions: 
    US (US and Oceania) | EU (Europe) | KR (Korea) | TW (Taiwan)

API call structure:
    https://{region}.api.blizzard.com/data/wow/connected-realm/{connected realm ID}/{requested data}/?namespace={name space type}&locale={language}&access_token={access token}

    Example (Con.realm 121 auctions):
        https://us.api.blizzard.com/data/wow/connected-realm/121/auctions?namespace=dynamic-us&locale=en_US&access_token=USaRGLkRHXgcMKV6oRaXo0bsFL6Siptzz4
